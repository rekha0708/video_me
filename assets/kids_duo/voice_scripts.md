# Kids Duo Voice Reference Scripts

Use these as starting scripts for clean 10-30 second reference takes. The goal is
voice identity and personality, not final episode dialogue.

## Max

Tone: enthusiastic, patient, warm, slightly older than Zoe.

```text
Hi Zoe, today we are learning a letter. Watch my finger draw a big line, then a little curve.
That makes the letter B. Great try. I like how you looked closely and said the sound with me.
Let's clap once, breathe, and try it again together.
```

## Zoe

Tone: confident toddler expert, playful, clear, proud but kind.

```text
Max, I know how to mix the batter. First we pour slowly, then we stir, stir, stir.
Oops, a little flour jumped out. That's okay. We can wipe it and keep going.
Look, the bowl is smooth now. I did it, and you helped me.
```
